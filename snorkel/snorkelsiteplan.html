Overview

Site Name

Kawaiahe Snorkel Club is the name chosen as it best explains what the site is about without too much descriptions

Purpose

To share our love for snorkeling sites and the snorkel club in Kawaihae Bay, providing a gallery and information about them.

Audience

People interested in snorkeling while visiting Kawaihae.

Individuals searching online via mobile devices or personal computers for Hawaii snorkeling opportunities.

They will find images and information about snorkeling in Kawaihae Bay, Hawaii.
