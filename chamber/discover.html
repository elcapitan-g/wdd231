Local Spots

Visit Hapuna beach, rated the top beach in the United States. Warm soft sand and crystal clear water awaits you!
Hike the Kohala volcano, home to towering waterfalls and lush green rainforests.
The town of Waimea is nestled between two large volcanos: Kohala and Mauna Kea.
Rich history and ancient myths fill the area, where King Kamehameha began his conquest of the islands.
Discover the ruins of the ancient temple Mo'okini, where the early inhabitants were first introduced to human sacrifice!
Local cows, horses, and other wildlife frolic in the rolling hills and streams of Waimea.
